# semlbci: Likelihood-Based Confidence Interval in Structural Equation Models

Forms likelihood-based confidence intervals (LBCIs) for parameters in
structural equation modeling, introduced in Cheung and Pesigan (2023)
[doi:10.1080/10705511.2023.2183860](https://doi.org/10.1080/10705511.2023.2183860)
. Currently implements the algorithm illustrated by Pek and Wu (2018)
[doi:10.1037/met0000163](https://doi.org/10.1037/met0000163) , and
supports the robust LBCI proposed by Falk (2018)
[doi:10.1080/10705511.2017.1367254](https://doi.org/10.1080/10705511.2017.1367254)
.
